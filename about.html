About me

Hi, I'm Rowan.

I'm a Senior UX Designer at Concord USA, currently embedded with Roche designing digital pathology instruments and software products used in clinical and research labs worldwide.

I bring together 7+ years of design experience, a passion for human-centred problem solving, and a background in psychology to create products that are both intuitive and impactful — especially in high-stakes medical environments where clarity and safety matter most.
